Cacio e Pepe

Description

Spaghetti cacio e pepe is a simple Italian pasta dish. It's made with pecorino cheese and black pepper. The dish is easy to make, yet delicious.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Cook the pasta until al dente.
  3. Mix pecorino cheese and black pepper in a bowl.
  4. Drain the pasta and add it to the bowl.
  5. Mix everything together until the cheese is melted.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
