Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti alla carbonara is a traditional Italian pasta dish. It's simple, yet delicious. The dish is made by mixing eggs, cheese, bacon, and black pepper with cooked pasta.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Guanciale
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Cook the pasta until al dente.
  3. While the pasta is cooking, mix eggs, cheese, and black pepper in a bowl.
  4. Cook the guanciale in a pan.
  5. Drain the pasta and add it to the pan with the guanciale.
  6. Remove the pan from the heat and add the egg mixture.
  7. Mix everything together until the eggs are cooked.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
