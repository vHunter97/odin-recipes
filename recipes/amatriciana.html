Amatriciana

Description

Spaghetti all'amatriciana is another traditional Italian pasta dish. It's made with tomatoes, guanciale, and pecorino cheese. The dish is simple, yet flavorful.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Tomatoes
  • Guanciale
  • Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Cook the pasta until al dente.
  3. Cook the guanciale in a pan.
  4. Add the tomatoes and red pepper flakes to the pan.
  5. Drain the pasta and add it to the pan.
  6. Add the pecorino cheese and mix everything together.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
